Kindle-friendly setup tips

Keep pages readable and responsive on e-ink.

  • Toggle grayscale: Dark mode can invert colors; if text looks faint, switch to light mode in the Kindle browser settings.
  • Resize text: Increase font size in-browser for less ghosting and smoother scrolling.
  • Full-screen view: Hide the toolbar for more space when typing in forms or notes.
  • Bookmark apps: Long-press the address bar to add individual apps to your bookmarks for one-tap access.
  • Offline awareness: These links require connectivity; avoid image-heavy pages to reduce load times.
